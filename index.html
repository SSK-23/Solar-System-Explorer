Planets

Mercury: Color: Silver. Smallest planet, with a thin atmosphere and extreme temperature variations.
Venus: Color: Pink. Similar in size to Earth but has a toxic atmosphere and surface temperatures hot enough to melt lead.
Earth: Color: Blue. The only known planet to support life, with diverse ecosystems and 70% of its surface covered by water.
Mars: Color: Red. Known as the Red Planet, it has the largest volcano (Olympus Mons) and evidence of past water.
Jupiter: Color: Orange. The largest planet, a gas giant known for its Great Red Spot, a massive storm that has lasted for centuries.
Saturn: Color: Gold. Famous for its stunning rings, Saturn is a gas giant with a unique appearance and many moons.
Uranus: Color: Violet. An ice giant with a unique tilted axis, it has a faint ring system and is mostly made of hydrogen and helium.
Neptune: Color: Green. The farthest planet from the Sun, Neptune has the fastest winds in the Solar System and is known for its vibrant storms.
Sun: The Sun is a star at the center of our Solar System. It provides light and heat that sustain life on Earth. Its surface is extremely hot, and it emits a wide spectrum of radiation.
